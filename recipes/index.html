How to make chocolate cake

INGREDIENTS

  • Flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Sugar
  • Baking soda
  • Baking Power
  • Espresso powder
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Chocolate Buttercream Frosting
  • Boiling Water



DIRECTIONS

1. Add flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, salt and espresso powder to a large bowl or the bowl of a stand mixer.
2. Whisk through to combine or, using your paddle attachment, stir through flour mixture until combined.
3. Mix in wet ingredients. Add milk, vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla to flour mixture and mix together on medium speed until well combined.
4. Reduce speed and carefully add boiling water to the cake batter until well combined.
5. Bake. Distribute cake batter evenly between the two prepared cake pans. Bake for 30-35 minutes, until a toothpick or cake tester inserted in the center of the chocolate cake comes out clean.
6. Frost. Remove from the oven and allow to cool for about 10 minutes, remove from the pan and cool completely. Frost with Chocolate Buttercream Frosting.
